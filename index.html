About Us

About Sadaqah Foundation

The Sadaqah Foundation is a non-political, non-profit organization dedicated to charity, education, da'wah and full human welfare.


The Foundation mainly works in three sectors: Charity, Service and da'wah.


Provide financial assistance for handicrafts, machinery, technical, farm management training as well as self-reliance in eliminating unemployment and alleviating poverty. Provide relief assistance to affected areas from time to time and during natural disasters.
